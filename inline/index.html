My favorite place in Indonesia

Kuta Beach

Kuta Beach in Bali is a popular destination known for its golden sands, great surf, and vibrant atmosphere. It's a hotspot for surfers and party enthusiasts, offering a lively nightlife scene with numerous bars and restaurants. The beach is energetic and bustling, making it a must-visit for those seeking a vibrant coastal experience in Bali.

  • price: Rp 2.000
  • location: Badung Regency
  • Rating: 9.8⭐

Things to bring:

  1. camera
  2. volleyball
  3. bucket
  4. mat
  5. cold drink

Bali Zoo

Bali Zoo is a captivating wildlife park in Indonesia, offering a diverse collection of exotic animals and a unique animal encounter experience. Nestled in the lush surroundings of Bali, the zoo provides an opportunity for visitors to get up close with a variety of species in a well-maintained and immersive environment. From interactive animal encounters to educational programs, Bali Zoo offers a memorable and family-friendly adventure, showcasing the beauty of Indonesia's wildlife.

  • price: Rp 50.000
  • location: Gianyar Regency
  • Rating: 7.7⭐

Things to bring:

  1. camera
  2. bag
  3. water
  4. candy
  5. hat

Monkey Forest

Monkey Forest, located in Ubud, Bali, is a captivating nature reserve and temple complex. Home to a large population of playful long-tailed macaques, the forest offers visitors a unique opportunity to observe these monkeys in their natural habitat. The sanctuary is not only a haven for wildlife enthusiasts but also features ancient temples and lush greenery, creating a serene and mystical atmosphere. Visitors can explore winding pathways, encounter the resident monkeys, and experience the cultural and natural richness of this iconic Balinese attraction.

  • price: Rp 20.000
  • location: Gianyar Regency
  • Rating: 10⭐

Things to bring:

  1. bananas
  2. water
  3. a sealed bag
  4. sunglasses
  5. guide
